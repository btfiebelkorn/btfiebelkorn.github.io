Passwords

Keeping passwords safe online is good cyber hygiene. Don't reuse passwords. Use stonger passwords. Look for good password managers.

Family Safe

Keeping children safe online is a goal of every parent. Blocking inappropriate websites, talking to kids about safety are good places to start. 

Opening Attachments

Always be suspicious of email attachments. Don't open an email attachment from an unknown source and verify the sender. Click title above to take a phishing quiz.

Identity protection

Make sure to keep all sensitive personal information as private as possilbe. This includes social security numbers, birthdates, and account numbers for sensitive information.

Wireless Security

Be careful when using open wireless networks at store & coffeeshops. Never use this type of connection to share sensitive information.

Online Shopping

Online shopping poses several cyber risks. Always use a credit card when shopping online and check the site for a secure https connection. Click "Online Shopping" for more info.
